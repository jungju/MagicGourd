# Adopted Design

## Chosen Direction
Add a **light escalation layer** on top of the existing post-ending replay errands.

## Why
This keeps the current design intact and adds value exactly where the loop is currently thinnest: after the player has already seen the branch-specific prompt once.

## Player-facing behavior
### Restoration
- Each shared-basket hand-in still costs 1 normal gourd.
- Base reward remains modest.
- Every third shared drop also returns 1 seed.
- Copy shifts from simple gratitude to the feeling that the valley is beginning to replant itself.

### Tribute
- Each road-due hand-in still costs 1 normal gourd.
- Base reward remains modest.
- Every third due grants 1 extra inspection coin.
- Copy shifts from basic compliance to the feeling that the road now expects and recognizes steady tribute.

## Presentation
- Keep using the existing effect popup and dialogue box.
- Normal hand-ins use current short feedback.
- Milestone hand-ins use stronger effect titles and custom dialogue.
- World signs and quest hint text should acknowledge the escalation once counts cross simple thresholds.

## Scope guardrails
- No new quest stage.
- No new persistent minigame.
- No rotating locations in this pass.
- No dominant money source.

## Validation
1. Reach `COMPLETE` on restoration and hand in 3 gourds.
2. Confirm the third hand-in also grants 1 seed and upgraded copy.
3. Reach `COMPLETE` on tribute and hand in 3 gourds.
4. Confirm the third hand-in also grants 1 extra coin and upgraded copy.
5. Confirm ordinary farming and existing ending errands still work between milestones.

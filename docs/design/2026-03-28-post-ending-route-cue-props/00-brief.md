# Brief

## Feature
Post-Ending Route Cue Prop Pass

## Why Now
The post-ending loop now already has the important structural pieces in place:
- branch-aware ambient aftermath
- repeatable branch errands
- rotating support points
- landmark accrual tiers
- village witness interactions

What still risks feeling thin is **physical legibility while moving through the village**. Players can follow signs and hints, but the active support point does not yet leave much of a subtle environmental breadcrumb trail.

## Desired Outcome
Add a tiny branch-aware route-cue layer so the currently active support point reads more naturally in-world:
- restoration cues should feel warm, communal, and quietly helpful
- tribute cues should feel orderly, official, and slightly restrictive

## Hard Constraints
- no new questline
- no new reward logic
- no heavy VFX spam
- no bespoke imported assets required
- keep props primitive/runtime-authored and easy to cut if readability drops

## Deliverable Goal
Produce a focused PM/Dev-ready design for a small route-cue prop pass that improves navigation and mood without bloating the replay loop.

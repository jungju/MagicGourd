# Feature Brief

## Name
Blessed Seed Payoff + Nolbu Retaliation

## Problem
The current story reaches the swallow-healing beat, then stalls before the famous magical reward and the next emotional turn. The player helps, but does not yet receive a true magic-gourd payoff or see Nolbu react.

## Desired Player Experience
- Heal the swallow and feel that kindness matters.
- Receive a visibly special blessed seed from Heungbu.
- Grow and harvest a true magic gourd with stronger feedback than the normal farming loop.
- Bring it to Heungbu and trigger a satisfying reward burst.
- See Nolbu respond with petty retaliation that creates a new short recovery task.

## Why Now
This is the highest-priority open P1 story blocker in PM. It also unlocks the next P1 slice naturally instead of adding disconnected polish.

## Constraints
- Roblox constraints: keep logic server-driven and avoid Studio-only sequencing.
- Rojo constraints: runtime props/prompts should remain under the existing runtime structure.
- Narrative constraints: preserve Heungbu kindness vs Nolbu greed.
- Scope constraints: deliver a playable vertical slice, not full ending branches.

## Existing Systems Touched
- quest stage progression
- farming patch interaction
- Heungbu / Nolbu dialogue
- HUD attributes
- runtime billboards and props

## Success Signal
A player can progress from swallow healing into blessed seed planting, harvest a magic gourd, receive a stronger payoff, trigger Nolbu retaliation, resolve the retaliation, and continue playably.

# Brief

## Feature
Post-ending Story Stone reflections

## Problem
The Story Stone is a strong thematic object in the intro, but after the ending it does not fully reflect the player's branch choice, current replay-state momentum, or the village's changing tone. The world signs update, but the central narrative prop still under-communicates the aftermath.

## Goal
Turn the Story Stone into a reliable branch-aware reflection point during `COMPLETE` so players can re-read what kind of valley they created and get a soft reminder of the current replay loop tone.

## Why now
This is a safe follow-up after route cues, witness barks, landmarks, and rare events. It improves visible player experience and narrative coherence without adding new quest state or fragile map dependencies.

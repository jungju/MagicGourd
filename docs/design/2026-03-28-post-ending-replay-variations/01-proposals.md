# Proposals

## Proposal A — Branch-Specific Village Errands
- **Problem:** Post-ending play currently changes atmosphere more than action.
- **Goal:** Add one lightweight repeatable errand per ending.
- **Player Feel:** The valley asks something different of you depending on what future you chose.
- **Implementation Difficulty:** Medium-low.

## Proposal B — Rotating Support-Point Restoration Tasks
- **Problem:** Heungbu yard recovery currently resolves once and then stays static.
- **Goal:** Rotate 2-3 tiny repair/support points after ending completion.
- **Player Feel:** You keep tending the valley's weak spots.
- **Implementation Difficulty:** Medium.

## Proposal C — Tribute Route Pressure Loop
- **Problem:** Tribute ending has tone, but little ongoing pressure beyond text.
- **Goal:** Add a tiny toll/inspection interaction around Nolbu's route.
- **Player Feel:** Tribute peace feels controlled and slightly oppressive.
- **Implementation Difficulty:** Medium-low.

## Proposal D — Reputation Ledger / Village Mood Meter
- **Problem:** Endings do not accumulate ongoing consequence.
- **Goal:** Track post-ending choices through a visible meter.
- **Player Feel:** Ongoing stewardship or compliance shapes the village.
- **Implementation Difficulty:** Medium-high.

## Proposal E — Rare Ending Echo Events
- **Problem:** The world lacks surprise after the ending.
- **Goal:** Occasionally trigger branch-specific ambient mini-events.
- **Player Feel:** The ending still breathes in the world.
- **Implementation Difficulty:** Medium-high because cadence/state handling can sprawl.

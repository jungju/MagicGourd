# Candidate Set

## Candidate 1 — Branch-Specific Village Errands
A compact repeatable layer with one restoration-flavored community delivery and one tribute-flavored toll/inspection hand-in. Uses existing prompts, patch harvest loop, and ending state.

## Candidate 2 — Rotating Support-Point Tasks
After ending resolution, one of a few small support markers appears (repair fence, clear bramble sprouts, restock seed basket). Reuses current world-marker approach.

## Candidate 3 — Tribute Route Pressure Loop
Focus only on the tribute ending with a slightly deeper repeatable road obligation mechanic. Strong tone, but asymmetric.

## Candidate 4 — Ending Echo Events
Branch-aware timed/rare events that briefly alter signs, dialogue, or prompts. High flavor, but more hidden complexity around cadence and resets.

## Direction Coverage
- **Systems / replay loop:** Candidate 1
- **World interaction / staging:** Candidate 2
- **Tone-specific systemic pressure:** Candidate 3
- **Presentation / ambient dynamism:** Candidate 4

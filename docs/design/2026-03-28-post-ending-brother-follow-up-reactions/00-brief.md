# Brief

## Feature
Post-Ending Brother Follow-Up Reactions

## Why now
The post-ending replay loop is now structurally mature in code:
- branch-aware aftermath
- repeatable branch errands
- support-point rotation
- landmark accrual tiers
- witness barks
- route cue props
- rare errand events
- Story Stone reflections

That means the next high-value gap is no longer routing or village-side readability. It is **core character acknowledgment**. The player can keep reinforcing the ending choice, but Heungbu and Nolbu still risk feeling too static after the main ending resolves.

## Problem
The replay loop now has better world response than brother response. That inverts the emotional priority of the story: side witnesses and signage can reflect repeated labor, but the two brothers do not yet consistently react to the player's continued support of restoration or tribute.

## Goal
Add a small, low-spam follow-up reaction layer for Heungbu and Nolbu in `COMPLETE` so repeated errands and higher landmark tiers feel personally acknowledged by the characters most tied to the ending choice.

## Constraints
- No new quest stages
- No new economy/reward track
- Must not drown out village witnesses or rare events
- Must reuse current prompt/dialogue patterns
- Must stay branch-aware and feel true to the brothers' personalities

## Success signal
After several post-ending errands, talking to Heungbu or Nolbu should feel meaningfully different depending on branch, active village state, and replay-loop progress, without turning them into mandatory repeat quest dispensers.

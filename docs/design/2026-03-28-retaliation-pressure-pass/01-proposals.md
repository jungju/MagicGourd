# Proposals

## Proposal A - Retaliation Pressure Sequence
- Summary: expand retaliation into a short 2-3 action recovery chain: inspect sabotage, clear brambles, restore one village support point, then return to Heungbu.
- Why it helps: preserves pacing while making Nolbu's counterattack feel real and actionable.
- Cost: medium.
- Risk: could overcomplicate the finale if too many steps are added.

## Proposal B - Patrol Hostility Escalation
- Summary: Nolbu's patrols become more aggressive around the patch after the miracle payoff.
- Why it helps: makes retaliation feel systemic and visible in the world.
- Cost: medium-high.
- Risk: pathfinding edge cases and potential frustration if patrol behavior blocks core quest readability.

## Proposal C - Social Recovery Beat
- Summary: after brambles, the player briefly helps villagers or props recover morale through one communal repair interaction.
- Why it helps: reinforces Heungbu's theme of shared recovery.
- Cost: medium.
- Risk: can feel disconnected if no villager system exists beyond signs/dialogue.

## Proposal D - Ending-Choice Retaliation Merge
- Summary: keep retaliation short but move most of its consequence into the final choice framing, with Nolbu's attack directly shaping the final audience copy.
- Why it helps: minimal new implementation.
- Cost: low.
- Risk: solves readability in writing only, not in actual play.

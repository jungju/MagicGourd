# Feature Brief

## Name
Retaliation Pressure Pass

## Problem
The current post-payoff retaliation beat lands as one Nolbu interaction and one bramble-clear action. It technically works, but the emotional arc is too short: the player gets a miracle, hears one threat, clears one obstacle, and is already at the final audience. That makes Nolbu's jealousy read more like a brief interruption than a meaningful story counter-force.

## Desired Player Experience
After the blessed harvest ceremony, the player should feel that Nolbu's spite actually disturbs the village for a short stretch. The recovery should still be lightweight and Roblox-safe, but it should ask the player to do more than click one bramble prompt before the finale.

## Why Now
- PM already identifies retaliation expansion as the next new work.
- Branching ending and post-ending ambience are implemented, so the weakest remaining story step is the transition from miracle to finale.
- This can strengthen the existing slice without inventing a new chapter or persistence system.

## Constraints
- Roblox constraints: no complex cutscene rails, no fragile NPC choreography, no heavy path/state explosion.
- Rojo constraints: runtime-authored props/prompts only; keep assets code-defined and diff-friendly.
- Narrative constraints: retaliation should express Nolbu's greed/control, but must not overshadow the Heungbu ending choice.
- Scope constraints: this is a compact mid-finale conflict pass, not a new economy or reputation system.

## Existing Systems Touched
- blessed payoff presentation
- Nolbu retaliation stage
- bramble / patch runtime props
- village signs / prompt copy
- Heungbu and Nolbu dialogue routing

## Success Signal
The retaliation section becomes a memorable short pressure sequence with 2-3 concrete actions, clearer village consequence, and a smoother handoff into the final audience.

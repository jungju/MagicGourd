# Proposals

## Proposal 1 — Ending Mood Layer
- **Problem:** ending choice resolves, but the village quickly feels the same again.
- **Goal:** let the whole village carry a persistent restoration-vs-tribute tone during free play.
- **Player feel:** "My decision actually changed this place."
- **Difficulty:** low-medium

## Proposal 2 — Post-Ending Repeatable Tasks
- **Problem:** free play after the ending has weak narrative framing.
- **Goal:** add one small repeatable interaction loop per ending.
- **Player feel:** "I am now supporting the village path I chose."
- **Difficulty:** medium

## Proposal 3 — Retaliation Escalation Echoes
- **Problem:** Nolbu's sabotage is a single beat with little lingering pressure.
- **Goal:** add recurring ambient signs of tension after the story climax.
- **Player feel:** "The conflict is still alive, even after the finale."
- **Difficulty:** medium

## Proposal 4 — Village Reaction Barks
- **Problem:** there are too few spoken consequences after the ending.
- **Goal:** give Heungbu, Nolbu, and signs/prompts branch-aware ambient lines.
- **Player feel:** "The characters remember what happened."
- **Difficulty:** low

## Proposal 5 — Ceremony-Style Ending Monuments
- **Problem:** the ending lacks a memorable visual anchor.
- **Goal:** add a dedicated shrine/banner/marker representing the chosen ending.
- **Player feel:** "There is a landmark that proves the story changed."
- **Difficulty:** medium

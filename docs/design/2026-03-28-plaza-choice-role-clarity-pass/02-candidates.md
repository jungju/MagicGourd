# Candidates

## Candidate A — Plaza Choice Role Clarity
Make each upgrade read as a distinct loop-specialist option.

Primary question answered:
- `Which upgrade should I buy, and what part of the loop does it improve?`

Expected benefit:
- clearer buy decisions
- better reward feel when spending money
- stronger coherence between plaza, HUD, and next run

---

## Candidate B — Pad Approach Commitment Follow-through
Further tighten the physical read of which plaza pad is active from ordinary approach positions.

Primary question answered:
- `Which pad am I really committing to right now?`

Expected benefit:
- less prompt ambiguity
- cleaner plaza movement
- stronger placement consistency

---

## Candidate C — Upgrade Payoff Afterglow
Author a small rule set for how a purchase should frame the next safe action window.

Primary question answered:
- `I bought something — what should feel different immediately after?`

Expected benefit:
- stronger progression feel
- less bookkeeping tone after purchases
- cleaner handoff back into the loop

---

## Candidate D — Danger Retreat Readability
Refine retreat/re-entry understanding when goblin slow lands or fades.

Primary question answered:
- `How do I safely reset after danger interrupts me?`

Expected benefit:
- better fairness perception
- clearer danger handling
- smoother recovery flow

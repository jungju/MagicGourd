# Brief

## Pass Name
Danger Boundary Re-entry Clarity Pass

## Why now
The current arcade loop already teaches:
- village heal
- plant / break farming
- plaza spending
- goblin danger pressure

What is less fully authored is the **handoff back out of danger**.

The player can understand that Nolbu Area is risky, but after a slow, retreat, or aborted push, the re-entry beat can still feel slightly loose:
- where the danger lane ends
- when the player is safely back in the loop
- whether the next intended move is break, plant, buy, or village reset

This is not a missing feature problem. It is a **boundary-read and return-flow precision** problem.

## Scope
Refine the existing danger-edge and safe-side handoff so retreat and recovery feel readable, fair, and coherent.

## Non-goals
- no new enemies
- no new tutorial layer
- no new UI surface
- no zone expansion
- no reward rebalance

## Deliverable
A low-scope design contract for danger-edge placement, retreat readability, recovery feedback ownership, and safe-side loop re-entry guidance.

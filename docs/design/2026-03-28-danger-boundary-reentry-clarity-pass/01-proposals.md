# Proposals

## Refine targets collected
1. danger boundary readability between safe center lane and Nolbu push lane
2. post-slow retreat handoff clarity once the player exits immediate pressure
3. safe-side route ownership so plaza / center lane / village fallback do not blur together after retreat
4. recovery feedback discipline so `slow faded` yields cleanly into a concrete next step
5. world landmark consistency for danger entry vs safe return
6. interaction competition near the danger-side edge if farming objects or plaza cues read too close to retreat space

## Proposal A — Boundary Landmark Clarification
Tighten signs, prop rhythm, and lane silhouette so the player can feel a distinct transition into and out of danger.

## Proposal B — Recovery-to-Reentry Guidance Contract
Formalize what the HUD / banner / popup stack should do during slow application, retreat, recovery, and first safe re-entry.

## Proposal C — Retreat Lane Spatial Budget
Define a small placement budget so the danger return lane stays visually separable from plaza commitment and center-lane farming activity.

## Proposal D — Combined Danger Re-entry Completion Pass
Combine the best parts of A, B, and C into one low-scope contract covering:
- boundary read
- retreat lane clarity
- recovery feedback ownership
- safe-side next-step restoration

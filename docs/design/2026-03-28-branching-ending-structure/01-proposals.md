# Proposals

## Proposal A
- Summary: add a post-bramble final audience where the player sides with Heungbu's restorative path or Nolbu's tribute-driven path.
- Why it helps: creates a real ending decision using existing NPCs and moral contrast.
- Cost: medium.
- Risk: can feel too binary if the setup is not grounded in prior actions.

## Proposal B
- Summary: make the ending branch purely score-based from kindness vs greed counters, with no explicit final decision prompt.
- Why it helps: preserves diegetic storytelling and rewards prior play.
- Cost: low-medium.
- Risk: players may not understand why they got a specific ending.

## Proposal C
- Summary: add a village restoration festival where the ending depends on whether the player restored hope fast enough after retaliation.
- Why it helps: gives a stronger celebratory finale and uses the current payoff presentation layer well.
- Cost: medium-high.
- Risk: needs more authored staging and may drift into spectacle before the core ending logic is solid.

## Proposal D
- Summary: split the current `COMPLETE` state into a short epilogue loop with repeatable favor tasks that gradually unlock one of several endings.
- Why it helps: increases longevity and makes endings feel earned over time.
- Cost: high.
- Risk: too large for the immediate next implementation window; likely to delay a clean ending altogether.
